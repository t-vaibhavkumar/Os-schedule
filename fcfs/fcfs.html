First-Come First-Serve (FCFS) Scheduling

Overview

First-Come First-Serve (FCFS) is the simplest scheduling algorithm. The process that arrives first is selected for execution first.

Advantages:

  • Simple and easy to implement.
  • No process starvation.

Disadvantages:

  • High average waiting time if processes have different burst times.
  • Not suitable for real-time systems.
